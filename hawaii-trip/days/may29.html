Transit Day — Last Flavors of Honolulu

One final taste of Hawaii before heading home.

✈️
Fly Kona → Honolulu (KOA to HNL)
Culture

Return flight from Kona International Airport (KOA) back to Honolulu. Book a morning flight to maximize your time in Honolulu before your mainland connection. The Kona airport is small and easy — arrive 60 minutes before departure.

Tip Check your mainland flight's departure time and work backward. If you have 4+ hours in Honolulu, it's worth leaving the airport. If less than 3 hours, stay airside after checking in for your onward flight.

Last Plate Lunch
Food

One final plate lunch. Rainbow Drive-In on Kapahulu Ave is the quintessential local spot — sliced beef or chicken katsu with two scoops of rice and mac salad, all for under $12. Or head to Zippy's, the Hawaii fast-casual institution, for chili rice or a beef stew plate. These are the places locals go, not tourists — savor the ordinariness of it.

Airport food The Honolulu airport has surprisingly decent local options in the pre-security food court — including good poke bowls, saimin, and shave ice. Better than most mainland airports.

🎨
Honolulu Museum of Art (if time allows)
Culture

One of the finest art museums in the Pacific — a beautiful 1927 building with a world-class collection of Asian, Pacific, and Hawaiian art. The Hawaiian paintings by Madge Tennent and John Kelly capture something essential about the islands. The courtyard café is lovely for a last coffee before heading to the airport.

Timing Only attempt this if your mainland flight departs after 5 PM. Allow 90 minutes at the museum plus 60 minutes to get to the airport (account for peak-hour traffic). The museum is 10 minutes from the airport.
