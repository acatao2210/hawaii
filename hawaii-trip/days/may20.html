Pearl Harbor National Memorial
Culture

One of the most powerful sites in the United States. The USS Arizona Memorial sits over the sunken hull of the battleship, which still leaks oil — called 'black tears' — into the harbor. The Visitor Center is free; the boat tour to the Arizona Memorial requires a timed-entry reservation. Plan at least 3 hours for the full experience including the museum.

Critical Reserve your boat tour tickets at recreation.gov well in advance — they release 60 days ahead and sell out fast in May. Arrive 30 minutes early. No bags allowed beyond the visitor center.

Makapu'u Lighthouse Trail
Hiking

One of the most rewarding easy-to-moderate hikes on Oahu. A paved 2-mile round trip switchback trail climbs to dramatic sea cliffs above the eastern tip of the island. At the top you'll see the Makapu'u Lighthouse, panoramic ocean views, and in May there's a good chance of spotting humpback whales still making their way north. On clear days you can see Molokai.

Timing Go before 1 PM to avoid the worst heat. The trail is entirely exposed — bring water and sunscreen. The tide pools at the base of the cliffs below are accessible via a separate short trail.

Waimanalo Beach
Beach

One of the longest stretches of white sand beach on Oahu, and far less crowded than Waikiki. The water is calm and turquoise, backed by the dramatic Ko'olau Mountains. Locals come here on weekends — it's the kind of beach that makes you want to move to Hawaii.

Nearby Grab shave ice at Ululani's in Kailua on your way back to Waikiki — it's arguably the best on the island (Uji ice cream base with li hing mui is the move).

Chinatown Bar Crawl
Nightlife Food

Honolulu's Chinatown after dark is one of the best bar scenes in the Pacific. Start with dinner at Senia (if you can get a reservation — one of Hawaii's best restaurants) or The Pig & The Lady. Then hit Bar Leather Apron for serious cocktails, Tchin Tchin for terrace drinks, and finish at Smith & Kings or M Nightclub if you're going late.

Reservation Senia is worth making a reservation for — book at opentable.com. If you can't get in, Livestock Tavern and Fete are excellent alternatives on the same block.
