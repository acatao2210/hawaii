Koko Head Crater Hike Hike ▾
Only 0.45 miles to the summit, but you'll climb 1,048 old railway tie stairs — 930 feet of elevation gain. Known locally as the "Stairmaster of Death," it's brutal but short. Stunning 360° views of Hanauma Bay, Honolulu, and the Pacific. Go at sunrise for best light and cooler temps. No reservation needed.
Very exposed with no shade — sunscreen and water essential. Extremely hot by 9 am.
