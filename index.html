Trip Overview

Ten nights across two of Hawai'i's most distinct islands — the layered, vibrant culture of O'ahu followed by the raw volcanic drama of the Big Island. Waikiki and Diamond Head give way to North Shore shave ice and Friday fireworks on the beach; Kona coffee and night-diving with manta rays lead to Kīlauea's active lava fields and the mist-shrouded valleys of the Hāmākua Coast. This is Hawai'i at full range.
